BambuHelper Web Flasher

Flash BambuHelper firmware to your ESP32 board in one click.

Before you start

  • Use Chrome or Edge on a desktop or laptop (Web Serial is not supported on Firefox, Safari, or mobile browsers).
  • Plug your ESP32 board into a USB port with a data cable (some cables are power-only).
  • Close any other program that might be holding the serial port (Arduino IDE serial monitor, PlatformIO monitor, etc.).

1. Pick your board

2. Install

A dialog will ask which serial port to use - pick the one that appears when you plug the board in.

3. After flashing

Your device boots into setup mode and shows the WiFi name and password right on the screen, like this:

  1. Join the BambuHelper-XXXX WiFi from your phone or laptop. Password: bambu1234.
  2. Open http://192.168.4.1 in a browser.
  3. Enter your home WiFi credentials and your printer details, then save.

The device reboots, joins your WiFi, and starts monitoring the printer.

Need more detail? See the full setup guide on GitHub.
